Why Choose Us

Explore Our Institute 


3 Decades Of Excellence


The Gyan Dhara Public Senior Secondary School has been delivering high quality education for nearly 16+ years in the state of Rajasthan. The journey started with Gyan Dhara Nursery School which laid a solid foundation by providing the right learning atmosphere to our toddlers. The group then progressed towards the establishment of a progressive learning arena, named Gyan Dhara Public School at Panchana Colony, Karauli, and later on Gyan Dhara Senior Secondary School at ShikarGanj, was established.




Experienced Teachers


Professional development of faculty members is a part of the basic policy structure of the group. Experience is valued and the focus is on the active involvement of the teachers in the scholastic and co-scholastic development of students. Teachers act as mentors and role-models for students to follow.




Worldclass Education


The Gyan Dhara group offers a canvas of curriculum including the RBSE and IB curriculums with technology . These are imparted through the use of latest technology including Smart Boards, Digital Learning Aids, Edu-Toys etc.




360Â° Assessments


All-round, holistic development of Gyan Dhara is ensured through a variety of academic and co-curricular activities including music, dance, dramatics, swimming, karate, sports, yoga and meditation. We offer the professional leap-start fit-kid programme from pre-primary to grade 5.




Safe Environment


All parents trust Gyan Dhara, with its safe, secure and joyous environment where the students are free to focus on learning without external pressures or stress. Security guards, periodic attendance, round-the-clock CCTV monitoring, GPS enabled transport system ensure a safe and secure campus.
